About Our Program

Many families in rural areas lack awareness about balanced nutrition and dietary needs. Poor eating habits lead to anemia, weakness, malnutrition, and preventable diseases.

Our Mission: Educate communities on healthy, affordable nutrition using local ingredients. We conduct hands-on workshops, cooking demonstrations, and provide personalized health counseling.

Navigate through our site to discover nutritious foods and find tailored advice for your health concerns.

What We Offer

Local, Nutritious Meals

Learn to cook healthy, affordable meals using locally-available ingredients rich in essential nutrients.

Interactive Workshops

Hands-on cooking demonstrations and family sessions where we teach simple, traditional healthy recipes.

Health Prevention & Care

Practical guidance to prevent and manage anemia, malnutrition, diabetes, high blood pressure, and more.
